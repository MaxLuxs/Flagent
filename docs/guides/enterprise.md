# Enterprise

Flagent Enterprise is the commercial edition for teams that need multi-tenancy, advanced security, automated rollouts, and dedicated support. It builds on the same open-source core: feature flags, A/B experiments, analytics, and crash ingestion.

## What is Flagent Enterprise?

- **Multi-tenant deployment** — On-premise or your cloud; isolated tenants with separate API keys and data.
- **SSO** — SAML and OIDC so users sign in with your identity provider.
- **RBAC** — Custom roles and permissions beyond the built-in admin/viewer model.
- **Audit log** — Who changed what and when; retention and filtering for compliance.
- **Smart Rollout** — Automated gradual rollouts with metrics-based steps and optional auto-rollback.
- **Anomaly Detection** — Alerts on degradation; optional automatic rollback when thresholds are breached.
- **Crash-by-flag analytics** — See crash rates per flag and variant; integration with Anomaly and Smart Rollout.
- **Slack notifications** — Configurable alerts (e.g. flag changes, anomalies) into your Slack workspace.
- **Dedicated support** — Direct channel (email/chat) and SLA options.

All of the above are part of the Enterprise build (see [Pricing and editions](pricing-and-editions.md) for the comparison table). No additional features are required to describe Enterprise; this page is the single place for the full capability list.

## For whom

- **Platform and infrastructure teams** that run feature flags and experiments for multiple products or teams.
- **Regulated industries** that need SSO, RBAC, audit trails, and on-premise or air-gapped deployment.
- **Organizations** that want safe rollouts (Smart Rollout, Anomaly) and crash-by-flag visibility without building it in-house.
